Project Type: Group/Class

Objective: Earth is not longer able to sustain any human life and there is the constant search for a new planet that will be able sustain the human race. After finding a planet there is only one problem there is a volcano. Now the objective is to make a world that will be able to sustain the human race by building around the danger of the volcano.

In this project there was a scenario where that Earth is no longer able to sustain any human life anymore and there is the constant search for another planet since that the human race can inhabit. Though there is only one planet that was found and there is also just one problem, there is a volcano in the center of where it is best suitable for the human race. There was a lot of brainstorming around this problem and the solution of the city of bridges to be the best solution out of them all. This will allow humans to be able to live over the volcano and continue their lifestyle. My group was responsible for making the Entertainment part of the sector and this was consisting of many different thing such as restaurants, movie theaters, and etc. I had created the restaurant by making one version of the restaurants and also creating some of the natural features as well such as the trees and more. This project was done so that one can use sheet metal in AutoDesk Inventor to make these parts out of paper later on as a physical model.

After this project I had learned the different skills that were needed for this project. We had learned Sheet metal to make sure that we are able to create the various flat patterns that were needed for the folding of the various parts of the world that were there and needed. In this project we had made the flat patterns for the various parts of the entertainment sector such as the Movie theater, resturants, and more. During this porject I had made the two parts of the resturant that was one design of the resturants that were in our sector and I was also responsible for making the tree for our sector for some natural beauty in out sector as well.
